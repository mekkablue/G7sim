    How to use & controls

    1. Drag & drop the console BIOS (the 1 KB o2rom.bin — cannot be bundled for copyright reasons). 2. Drag & drop your games .zip (or a single .bin cartridge). 3. Click a game in the library. Everything is remembered for next time and runs fully offline — no ROM ever leaves your machine.

    Joystick (P1)Arrow keys · fire = Space or Left Shift
    Joystick (P2)Numpad 8462 · fire = Numpad 0
    KeypadNumber/letter keys map to the Videopac membrane keyboard; Enter, Backspace (DEL)

    CPU: Intel 8048 · Video: Intel 8244/8245 VDC. Emulation core reimplemented in JavaScript after the free O2EM project. Palette, character ROM and timing reproduce original hardware.
