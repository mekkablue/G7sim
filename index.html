    Help

    1 · Install the ROMs

    No BIOS or games are bundled (copyright), so you supply them once — everything stays on your machine and runs offline.

    • Console BIOS — drag & drop the 1 KB o2rom.bin anywhere on the page (G7400 / C52 / Jopac BIOSes are recognised too).
    • Games — drop a .zip of cartridge dumps, or a single .bin/.rom.
    • Or use 📁 Load files… instead of dragging.
    • When served over http(s), rom/rom.bin and rom/games.zip next to index.html load automatically. Your dropped files are saved and take precedence.

    2 · Open a game

    Pick a title from the Game library on the right. Use Reset to restart it and Pause to halt/continue.

    Controls

    Joystick 1Arrow keys · fire = Space
    Joystick 2Numpad 8462 · fire = Numpad 0
    KeyboardLetter & number keys map to the Videopac membrane keyboard; Enter, Backspace = DEL.
    Hi-scoresHold Shift with a letter to type it even when that letter is a joystick key.

    All joystick keys are rebindable in ⚙ Settings.

    Shortcuts

    Modifier is Option on macOS, AltGr on Windows.

    Alt+PPause / resume
    Alt+SSound on / off
    Alt+0Fullscreen
    Alt+Shift+JFlip joysticks
